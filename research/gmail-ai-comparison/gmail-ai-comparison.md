### Premium Email Clients with Advanced Auto-Labeling

#### 1. Superhuman

**Auto-Labeling Capability**: Exceptional - Features both standard "Auto Labels" and "Custom Auto Labels with AI"[^1][^2][^3]

**How It Works**: AI-powered automatic categorization that learns from your behavior. The Business plan (\$40/month) includes advanced "Custom Auto Labels" where AI can create sophisticated labeling rules.[^1]

**Labeling Features**:

- Split Inbox that automatically organizes messages
- AI-generated auto-label library with pre-built categories
- Custom auto-labels that you can define with natural language
- Labels apply in real-time as emails arrive

**Pricing**: \$30/month (Starter), \$40/month (Business), Custom (Enterprise). Annual billing reduces to \$25/\$33/month respectively.[^4][^5][^1]

**Draft Writing**: Yes - Instant Reply, Auto Drafts, Voice and Tone Match, Ask AI feature[^3][^1]

**Unique Features**:[^6][^1]

- **Collision detection**: Prevents two team members from replying to the same email
- **Recent Opens feed**: See when people open your emails in real-time
- **Read receipts**: Track email engagement
- **Keyboard-first design**: Ultra-fast navigation
- Requires 1-on-1 onboarding (which adds friction but ensures mastery)

**Best For**: Professionals willing to invest in premium tooling who handle 100+ emails daily and value speed.[^2]

#### 2. Shortwave

**Auto-Labeling Capability**: Excellent - AI filters with custom prompts[^7][^8][^9]

**How It Works**: Creates AI-powered filters where you write natural language prompts describing what should be labeled. For example: "Label emails about project deadlines as 'Urgent Project'" and Shortwave's AI will analyze content to apply labels.[^8][^10]

**Labeling Features**:[^9][^8]

- **AI filters**: 3 on Business plan, 10 on Premier, 50 on Max
- **Auto-apply labels**: One-click setup for specific senders
- **Bundles**: Automatically groups related emails (newsletters, promotions, etc.)
- **AI inbox organization**: One-click command to categorize entire inbox

**Pricing**: Free (limited), \$7/month (Personal), \$14/month (Pro), \$24/month (Business), \$36/month (Premier)[^7][^8][^9]

**Draft Writing**: Yes - Personalized "Ghostwriter" that learns your writing style, AI autocomplete[^11][^8]

**Unique Features**:[^10][^8][^11]

- **Natural language search**: "Find emails about the budget from last quarter" works perfectly
- **Todos built into email**: Convert emails to tasks without leaving inbox
- **AI searches across 5 years of history** (unlimited on Premier/Max)
- **Significantly cheaper than Superhuman** with similar capabilities
- Self-serve onboarding (no required training call)

**Best For**: Gmail power users who want sophisticated AI labeling at reasonable prices. Strong choice for your technical background.[^11]
