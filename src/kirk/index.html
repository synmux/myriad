Hate Crime Statistics (2022)

Discriminatory rhetoric contributes to a climate where hate crimes flourish:

  • 2,430 racially motivated incidents against Black Americans
  • 1,402 incidents targeting LGBTQ+ individuals
  • 1,124 incidents against Jewish people (60% of religious hate crimes)
  • 158 incidents targeting Muslims
  • 247 incidents based on national origin
  • 92 incidents based on disability

The Amplification Effect

When public figures with large platforms make discriminatory statements, they:

  • Normalize prejudice and hatred
  • Embolden others to express similar views
  • Create hostile environments for targeted communities
  • Contribute to political polarization
  • Undermine democratic values of equality and inclusion
